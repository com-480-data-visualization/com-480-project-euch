Olympic Athletes and their Morphologies

Olympics Games are composed of various sport, from marathon to gymnastics and all these sports require different physiques.
You can visualize the average height, weight and age by hovering your mouse over the bubbles.
You can also move to bubbles to rearrange them as you like.

Let's go a little deeper... Each sport is declined in several disciplines.
You can now select the sport, the discipline and the time window for computing the average physique of the athletes.
The little athlete figure change to represent your selection. The HIGHER and the larger the figure is, the taller and the heavier the athletes are. The age is depicted with the color, the lighter the younger.
You can also save a figure by adding it to the grid, so that you can compare different disciplines side-by-side.
